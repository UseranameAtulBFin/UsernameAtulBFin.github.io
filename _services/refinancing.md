# Is It Time to Refinance?

Refinancing means replacing your current loan with a new one — often with a lower rate, better features, or more flexibility. It's one of the most effective ways to put money back in your pocket.

## Reasons to Refinance

- **Lower your interest rate** — even a small reduction can save significantly over time
- **Access equity** — use built-up equity for renovations, investments, or other goals
- **Consolidate debt** — roll multiple debts into a single, lower-rate loan
- **Better loan features** — offset accounts, redraw facilities, or flexible repayments
- **Change your loan type** — switch from variable to fixed, or vice versa

## The Refinancing Process

1. **Loan health check** — Atul reviews your current loan and compares it to the market
2. **Gap analysis** — we identify what you could save and what features you're missing
3. **Lender search** — we source competitive options across our panel of 40+ lenders
4. **Seamless transition** — we handle the paperwork to make switching as smooth as possible

## Potential Costs to Consider

Refinancing isn't always free — there may be exit fees from your current lender or upfront costs with the new one. Atul will give you a clear picture of the numbers so you can make an informed decision.
