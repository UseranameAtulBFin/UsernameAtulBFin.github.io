Property investment is one of the most powerful ways to build long-term wealth in Australia. FinXP Services helps investors structure their finance strategically — maximising borrowing capacity and setting up for portfolio growth from day one.

# Grow Your Property Portfolio

Whether you're buying your first investment property or adding to an existing portfolio, the right loan structure makes a significant difference to your returns and future borrowing power.

## Investment Loan Solutions

- **Interest-only loans** — manage cash flow during the growth phase
- **Principal & interest** — build equity and reduce debt over time
- **Line of credit** — leverage existing equity to fund your next purchase
- **Portfolio lending** — structure multiple properties under a coordinated strategy

## Strategic Loan Structuring

The way your investment loans are set up matters. Atul looks at the bigger picture — not just the rate, but how each loan fits within your overall portfolio strategy, tax position, and future goals.

## What to Consider

- Borrowing capacity across multiple properties
- Negative and positive gearing implications
- Cross-collateralisation risks
- Lender policy on investment lending
