# Commercial Property Finance

Commercial property loans operate differently from residential lending — they often involve different LVR limits, shorter loan terms, and more detailed financial assessment. Atul brings clarity to the process and helps you navigate the options.

## Types of Commercial Property We Finance

- **Retail and office premises** — owner-occupied or investment
- **Industrial and warehouse** — logistics, storage, and light manufacturing
- **Strata office units** — smaller commercial spaces with competitive financing options
- **Mixed-use properties** — residential and commercial components combined

## Key Differences from Residential Lending

- **LVR typically up to 70–75%** — higher deposits generally required
- **Shorter loan terms** — commonly 15–25 years vs residential 30-year terms
- **Full financial disclosure** — lenders require detailed business financials
- **Business and personal assessments** — both the borrower and the asset are assessed

## Who This Is For

- Business owners looking to buy their own premises
- Property investors diversifying into commercial assets
- Existing commercial property owners seeking better rates or refinancing

Atul works with a range of commercial lenders to match your needs and help you move efficiently through what can be a complex process.
