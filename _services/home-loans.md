Finding the right home loan can feel overwhelming — but it doesn't have to be. Atul Bhatia works with a panel of 40+ lenders to find a loan that suits your financial situation, lifestyle goals, and long-term plans.

# What We Help With

Whether you're buying your first home or upgrading to your next, FinXP Services guides you through every step of the process.

## Loan Types We Source

- **Variable rate loans** — flexibility with the ability to make extra repayments
- **Fixed rate loans** — certainty on repayments for a set period
- **Split loans** — a mix of fixed and variable to get the best of both
- **Offset accounts** — reduce the interest you pay while keeping access to your funds
- **Redraw facilities** — access extra repayments when you need them

## The Process

1. **Free consultation** — We discuss your goals, income, and borrowing capacity
2. **Loan comparison** — Atul searches across lenders to find your best options
3. **Application support** — We handle the paperwork and liaise with the lender
4. **Approval and settlement** — We keep you informed right through to the finish line

A broker isn't tied to one bank. Atul has access to dozens of lenders — including options you won't find by walking into a branch — and the expertise to match you with the right product, not just the most convenient one.
