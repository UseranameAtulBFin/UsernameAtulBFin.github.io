# Finance for Building and Renovating

Unlike a standard home loan, a construction loan releases funds in stages as your build progresses. This means you only pay interest on what's been drawn down — keeping costs manageable while the work is underway.

## How Construction Loans Work

Funds are released in progressive drawdowns aligned to construction milestones:

1. **Slab / foundation** — first drawdown once the base is laid
2. **Frame** — walls and roof frame complete
3. **Lock-up** — windows, doors, and external walls in place
4. **Fit-out** — internal fixtures, fittings, and finishes
5. **Completion** — final inspection and handover
