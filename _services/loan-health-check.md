When did you last review your home loan? If it's been more than two years, chances are there's a better deal available. A FinXP loan health check is free, takes less than 30 minutes, and could save you thousands.

# Free Loan Health Check

Atul Bhatia reviews your existing loan against the current market to identify whether you're still on a competitive rate — and whether your loan structure still suits your needs.

## What We Review

- **Your current interest rate** — and how it compares to the best available rates
- **Loan features** — are you using your offset account, redraw, or other features effectively?
- **Repayment structure** — are you paying principal and interest, or interest only?
- **Loan term** — how long do you have remaining, and is that the right timeline for you?
- **Overall loan strategy** — does your loan still align with your goals?

## What You'll Receive

After the health check, Atul provides a clear summary of your current position and, if relevant, options for improvement. There's no pressure and no obligation — just honest advice.

## When to Get a Health Check

- You haven't reviewed your loan in over 12–24 months
- Interest rates have moved and you're unsure if you're still competitive
- Your circumstances have changed (new job, growing family, property purchase)
- You're thinking about accessing equity for renovations or investment
- You've seen low-rate advertised offers and want to know if they apply to you
