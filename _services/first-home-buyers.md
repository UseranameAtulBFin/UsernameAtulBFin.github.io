## Government Incentives for First Home Buyers

There are several grants and schemes available to eligible first home buyers in Australia:

- **First Home Owner Grant (FHOG)** — a one-off grant for eligible first home buyers
- **First Home Guarantee (FHBG)** — buy with as little as 5% deposit, without paying Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI)
- **Help to Buy Scheme** — shared equity support from the government
- **Stamp duty concessions** — vary by state; Atul will help you understand what applies to you
